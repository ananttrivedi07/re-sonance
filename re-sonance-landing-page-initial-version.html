Empowering lives touched by dementia

Reconnecting Memories, Rebuilding Lives

Re-sonance is dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for those affected by dementia through innovative, AI-powered solutions that foster connection and support.

The Problem We're Solving

Early-mid stage dementia patients

  • Feeling disconnected from family and events
  • Struggling with declining memory
  • Finding it difficult to utilize technology
  • Limited access to personalized support

Informal caregivers

  • Emotional burden and stress
  • Time constraints for proper care
  • Lack of specialized knowledge and training
  • Difficulty maintaining meaningful connections

Our Solution

AI-Powered Connection Platform

A smart solution that uses AI technology to create accessible interfaces that help reconnect patients with their memories and loved ones through familiar interactions.

For early-mid stage dementia patients

  • Familiar and intuitive interfaces
  • Memory enhancement tools
  • Simplified communication channels
  • Engaging yet calming content

For informal caregivers

  • Reduced care burden
  • Real-time monitoring capabilities
  • Training and support resources
  • Facilitated meaningful interactions

Our Technology

AI-driven personalization

Our platform adapts to each user's specific needs, preferences, and cognitive capabilities, providing a truly personalized experience.

Familiar interfaces

We utilize designs that resonate with the familiar experiences of dementia patients, reducing cognitive load and increasing comfort.

Adaptive digital interfaces

Our interfaces respond to user behavior and adjust accordingly to maintain engagement while preventing frustration.

Regular care and connection

The platform facilitates consistent communication and care routines that are vital for dementia management.
